a desktop grade calculator

What is Gradeulator?

It is essentially a desktop calculator that calculates your grades for you. The name comes from the combination of Grade + Calculator which combines to make Gradeulator.

Why?

I find it convenient to have a grade calculator ready on my desktop, so I decided to make one. Although there are quite a few *good* online calculators with the same function.

How do I use it?

Put your grade (marks) in the blue column, and put the corresponding weighting of the grade (e.g 5 percent would be entered as 5) in the red column, and press the caluculate button.
